Voortrekker through and through

Liam was elected the junior youth leader two days before he died. He was so proud of himself and I wish you could have seen him live when his name was announced.

The day he died his achievement was acknowledged at school and that picture shows him standing "tall" and proud. We were so proud of him as well. He would have been a fabulous leader. He was made to lead even though he was shy sometimes. He took responsibility to serve others very seriously.

Good job Liam!
