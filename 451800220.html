The Liam Sit

He started sitting on his haunches from very, very young. He could sit like that for hours, sometimes just holding his knees and sometimes doing something as well.

He also at some stage for about two years, he would bent forwards and then stare at you through his legs. Then for about 3-4 years he kept on doing cartwheels everywhere instead of walking. He also ran on his arms and legs on four paws and boy was he fast like that.

My Liam had all these funny quirks that I miss so very much!

Our lives are now boring, nothing interesting and fun-like Liam ever happens anymore. How are we suppose to never have new memories like these?
